Can you help me lose weight/body fat?

Absolutely! With my experience in personal training and my nutrition education, I have a lot of experience with helping clients achieve weight loss goals. However, with my coaching you will get much more than just a weight loss plan. Many times, my clients find that weight loss is not really the aspect of wellness that is the most important to them. During the Wellness Consultation, we will be able to identify what your true goals and priorities are and develop and action plan to help you reach them, whether or not they include weight loss.

Will you write me a meal plan?

Maybe! It depends on what we decide together in the wellness consultaion. Depending on your personality and goals, a meal plan may or may not be appropriate. I will absolutely help you with nutrition related issues, but I a meal plan is not always the most effective solution.

Will you write me a workout plan?

Maybe! Just like with the meal plans, a workout plan may or may not benefit you depeding on your goals and personality. During the wellness consultation, we will talk about what stratagies will benefit you most in reaching your health and wellness goals.
