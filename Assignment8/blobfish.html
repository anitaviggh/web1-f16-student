Save the Blobfish!

Everyone wants to save the pandas and tigers and rhinos, but what about everything else? When it comes to conservation, charismatic megafauna get the most attention. A charismatic megafauna is an animal that appeals to human aesthetic preferences by being expressive, majestic, fluffy, or otherwise beautiful. But what about the slimy, creepy, ugly, and funny looking members of the animal kingdom? They are just as important when it comes to maintaining biodiversity. If we want to keep our planet healthy, we need to give the ugly ones some love too.

Save the Blobfish! is an informational booklet all about those weird and wacky endangered animals that need a little more attention.
