About Annie

Annie Viggh is a graphic design student currently attending MassArt. Her main interest lie in web and app design, but she also does branding, print design, and illustration. Her programming experience includes HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, and some PHP. She fancies herself a novelist, but not a very good one. Besides creative work, her interests include aliens, waffles, and fixing people's obscure technical photoshop problems.
