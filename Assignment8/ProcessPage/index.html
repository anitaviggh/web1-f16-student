Goals

I want my portfolio site to serve a similar function and purpose as a typical Behance portfolio, but also show that I am capable of programming a functional website. Because I haven't done official work for clients yet, it will be to primarily showcase my work made in school. The focus will be on the projects themselves. I will need to show most of my acceptable work because I don't have a huge portfolio, but I will emphasize my most successful projects to make a good first impression.
